About AI Exam Simulator

AI Exam Simulator is a smart online platform designed to help students prepare for competitive exams like TNPSC, SSC, Railway, UPSC, and others. We simulate real exam conditions with topic-based practice and AI-generated questions in Tamil and English.

Key Features

  • 🔥 AI-powered question generation
  • 📝 Practice mode by topic & section
  • 🕒 Mock exams with real-time timer
  • 📊 Instant feedback & weak area detection
  • 🌐 Bilingual support: Tamil & English

Supported Exams

  • JEE – MAIN,ADVANCED.
  • NEET – Biology', 'Chemistry', 'Physics.
  • GATE – CSE', 'ECE', 'EEE', 'Mechanical', 'Civil.

Our Mission

To make exam preparation simple, smart, and accessible to every student in India – especially in Tamil Nadu – using the power of AI and open tools.
